# About HPE Labs

HPE Labs is a technical knowledge site focused on hands-on guidance for HPE infrastructure automation.

## What you will find here

- Practical walkthroughs for HPE GreenLake and Compute Ops Management
- PowerShell and API-first automation examples
- SSO/SAML integration guidance and operational best practices
- Reusable snippets and lab-style implementation patterns

## Scope

Content is designed for engineers, architects, and operations teams who want to automate day-to-day infrastructure workflows with repeatable, production-oriented approaches.

## Source and updates

This site is maintained in the hpelabs GitHub repository and continuously updated with new labs and implementation notes.
