## Connecting to the lab environment

To access the HPE Compute BU Enablement Environment, we will use VMware Horizon. Follow these steps:

1. Using your Chrome browser, navigate to the appropriate URL based on your network location to open the Horizon Access Portal:

   - **External to HPE** (not connected to HPE VPN): <https://labs.compute.cloud.hpe.com>

   - **Internal to HPE** (or connected to HPE VPN): <https://techenablement.hpecorp.net>

2. On the Horizon login screen, click the **Omnissa Horizon Web Client** button.

    [![]( {{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/HOLs/CSUS3200-Web-UI/2-HorizonSplash.png){: .bordered-image-thin}]( {{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/HOLs/CSUS3200-Web-UI/2-HorizonSplash.png){:class="img-600"}{: data-lightbox="gallery"}

3. Login with the credentials provided in your login sheet.

    [![]( {{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/HOLs/CSUS3200-Web-UI/3-HorizonLogin.png){: .bordered-image-thin}]( {{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/HOLs/CSUS3200-Web-UI/3-HorizonLogin.png){:class="img-600"}{: data-lightbox="gallery"}

4. Click on the graphic that represents your Lab environment.

    [![]( {{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/HOLs/CSUS3200-Web-UI/4-HorizonLabIcon.png){: .bordered-image-thin}]( {{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/HOLs/CSUS3200-Web-UI/4-HorizonLabIcon.png){:class="img-600"}{: data-lightbox="gallery"}

    Since Horizon is presenting a remote desktop session inside of your desktop's browser, it may be helpful to hit **F11** key at this time to set the browser in full screen mode.

## Start the simulator

1. To launch the simulator, click twice on the `ClickMe.bat` icon.

    [![]( {{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/HOLs/CSUS3200-Web-UI/5-ClickMe.png){: .bordered-image-thin}]( {{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/HOLs/CSUS3200-Web-UI/5-ClickMe.png){:class="img-30"}{: data-lightbox="gallery"}

2. A `CMD.exe` terminal is started, launching two Python windows and the defautl browser pointing the CSUS simulator:

    [![]( {{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/HOLs/CSUS3200-Web-UI/6-StartingSimulator.png){: .bordered-image-thin}]( {{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/HOLs/CSUS3200-Web-UI/6-StartingSimulator.png){:class="img-700"}{: data-lightbox="gallery"}

## Access & login

If you have several CSUS 3200 systems in your data centre, you can customize the background of each of their graphical user interfaces to better identify them.

Modify the splash screen background by clicking the `Choose a background image` button.

[![]( {{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/HOLs/CSUS3200-Web-UI/7-ChooseABackgroundImage.png){: .bordered-image-thin}]( {{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/HOLs/CSUS3200-Web-UI/7-ChooseABackgroundImage.png){:class="img-400"}{: data-lightbox="gallery"}

If not already done, **maximize** the size of your browser by clicking the **F11** key
to avoid any vertical or horizontal scroll-bar.

1. Select a background image

2. Click on the Information icon

3. Click on “Learn more”

  [![]( {{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/HOLs/CSUS3200-Web-UI/8-SelectBackground.png){: .bordered-image-thin}]( {{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/HOLs/CSUS3200-Web-UI/8-SelectBackground.png){:class="img-500"}{: data-lightbox="gallery"}
