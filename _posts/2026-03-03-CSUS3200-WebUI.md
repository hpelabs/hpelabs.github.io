## Connecting to the lab environment

To access the HPE Compute BU Enablement Environment, we will use VMware Horizon. Follow these steps:

1. Using your Chrome browser, navigate to the appropriate URL based on your network location to open the Horizon Access Portal:

   - **External to HPE** (not connected to HPE VPN): <https://labs.compute.cloud.hpe.com>

   - **Internal to HPE** (or connected to HPE VPN): <https://techenablement.hpecorp.net>

2. On the Horizon login screen, click the **Omnissa Horizon Web Client** button.

    [![]( {{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/HOLs/CSUS3200-Web-UI/2-HorizonSplash.png){: .bordered-image-thin}]( {{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/HOLs/CSUS3200-Web-UI/2-HorizonSplash.png){:class="img-600"}{: data-lightbox="gallery"}

3. Login with the credentials provided in your login sheet.

    [![]( {{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/HOLs/CSUS3200-Web-UI/3-HorizonLogin.png){: .bordered-image-thin}]( {{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/HOLs/CSUS3200-Web-UI/3-HorizonLogin.png){:class="img-600"}{: data-lightbox="gallery"}

4. Click on the graphic that represents your Lab environment.

    [![]( {{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/HOLs/CSUS3200-Web-UI/4-HorizonLabIcon.png){: .bordered-image-thin}]( {{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/HOLs/CSUS3200-Web-UI/4-HorizonLabIcon.png){:class="img-600"}{: data-lightbox="gallery"}

    Since Horizon is presenting a remote desktop session inside of your desktop's browser, it may be helpful to hit **F11** key at this time to set the browser in full screen mode.
